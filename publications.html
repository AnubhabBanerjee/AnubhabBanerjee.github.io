In this webpage you'll find information about my past and present research interests along with the list of my publications. If you have any question regarding my research methodology or results of my previous publications, feel free to contact me.

Research interests

My principal research area is network management automation. My principal research interest is applying game theory and artificial intelligence (machine learning and deep learning) for mobile network automation in 5G and beyond.
