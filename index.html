All about me

Currently I am doing my master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering Department of Technical University of Munich (TUM), Germany. I completed my Bachelor's of Engineering (B.E.) from Jadavpur University, Kolkata (2011 - 2015) with first class honors. After that, I worked in a software company for one year as a web based application developer before starting my Master's degree at TUM. Along with my study, I have been also working as a student researcher in Department of Informatics, TUM. I recently finished my thesis on content placement in connected edge networks under the supervision of Dr. Nishanth Sastry and Dr. Carmen Mas Machuca. Over a period of time, I have also continued research works remotely with Dr. Anand Seetharam on Information Centric Networking.
