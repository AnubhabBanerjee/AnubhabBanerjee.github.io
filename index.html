All about me

I am currently working as a Senior Research Specialist at Nokia, Munich, Germany since January 2023. My primary research focus includes application of Game Theory, Machine Learning, particularly Generative AI and LLM in mobile network management. I also regularly participate in mobile network standardization activities including 3GPP SA5 and O-RAN Alliance.

I obtained my Bachelors in Engineering (B.E.) degree from Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Jadavpur University, India in 2015 with First Class Honours. I finished my master's degree (M.Sc.) from Electrical and Computer Engineering Department of Technical University of Munich (TUM) in 2019 and my PhD degree (Dr.-Ing) from the Department of Informatics, TUM, in 2024. To know more about my current activities, feel free to check my website or contact me.
